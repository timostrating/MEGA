Opmerkingen

The courses Mathematics in the Life Sciences and C++ for Biologists are compulsory for people who do not already have sufficient knowledge of mathematics or programming.

If you already have sufficient knowledge of mathematics and/or programming and you want to do a full minor of 30 ECTS, you will have to find replacement courses.

Students are allowed to pick only one or two courses from this minor rather than completing the entire minor, provided they meet the entry requirements for the course(s) they're interested in.
