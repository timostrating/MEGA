Opmerkingen

The track Mathematics and Complex Dynamical Systems comprises of 120 ects:
- 15 ects compulsory courses: Complexity and Networks, Mathematical Modelling Colloquium, Mathematics and its Environment(jointly for all MSc Mathematics and Applied Mathematics students)
- (at least) 25 ects track specific courses: choose five courses from elective group A, the student can also choose track relevant courses from the Mastermath program (in consultation with the board of examiners).
- (at least) 15 ects of 'Guided Choice': modules from the lists of track specific courses of any of the tracks in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics or the Mastermath Programme.
- (at most) 15 ects of ‘Free Choice’: modules on Master level, relevant for the master Mathematics (at the discretion of the Board of Examiners).
- 50 ects Master Research Project

This module will take place in two fases.

1. Theory Science and Business
2. Group project Science and Business

In the first three weeks you will follow an intensive program in which you get acquainted with important business concepts. After that you will train the skills to solve business cases and to write and present a feasible science advise to company owners. Most assignments will be done in groups and teams.
