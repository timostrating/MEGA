11Topclass I: recent developmentsGKTDIT1-11
Introduction in recent developments in medical, biomedical and pharmaceutical sciences. All recent scientific developments discussed in this class are linked to the cell and cellular processes.
Therefore this top class starts with a refresh course of two weeks on the molecular biology of the cell.

To provide students with an overview of several techniques used in medical, biomedical and pharmaceutical research. Students will be able to differentiate between these techniques based on advantages and disadvantages in a specific research setting and for several types of research questions.

13Topclass III:Theoretical preparationGKTDIT3_4
In this course students will be introduced to the different research topics within the participating research institutes.
Furthermore students will choose the topic for their first research project and will defend this choice in front of the Board of Examiners. Also, students will have research track- group meetings during RPI.
