4MHIR: Specialisatie 1LGR013M10
The research internship can be carried out at a research institute in Groningen or elsewhere in the Netherlands, or (preferably) at a research institute abroad. The student carries out his or her own small research project, or actively participates in a larger research project carried out by the local research group. As an alternative to an Internship students can take extra courses e.g. at the Dutch National Research Schools. During this specialization phase the students writes three (Tutorials) papers each worth 10 ECTS (combinations of modules are possible). One of the tutorials will be presented during the winter/summer seminars of the ReMa.

6Research Master's Thesis MHIRLGR998M30
In the context of the Research Master's degree, students will write a thesis in the area of their chosen specialization. Theses are then discussed in class. Students receive feedback on their work and have the opportunity to discuss questions and problems with each other. These meetings will also be used to comment on research proposals and to practice writing research proposals.
